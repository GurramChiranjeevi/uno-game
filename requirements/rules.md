# UNO Game Rules

## Objective

Be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

---

# Setup

- 2–4 players
- Shuffle the deck
- Deal 7 cards to each player
- Place remaining cards face down as the draw pile
- Flip the top card to begin the discard pile

---

# Turn Rules

On your turn, you must do ONE of the following:

1. Play a matching card
2. Play a Wild card
3. Draw a card

---

# Matching Rules

You can play a card if it matches the top discard card by:

- Color
- Number
- Symbol / Action

### Example

Top card = `Red 5`

Valid plays:
- `Red 2`
- `Blue 5`
- `Red Skip`
- `Wild`

---

# Drawing Cards

If you cannot play:
- Draw 1 card

If the drawn card is playable:
- you may play it immediately

Otherwise:
- your turn ends

---

# Action Cards

## Skip Card

### Effect
- Next player's turn is skipped

---

## Reverse Card

### Effect
- Reverses turn direction

### Example
- clockwise → counterclockwise

### Special Case (2 Players)
Reverse acts like Skip.

---

## Draw Two Card

### Effect
- Next player draws 2 cards
- Next player loses their turn

---

# Wild Cards

## Wild Card

### Effect
- Choose the next color

### Rule
- Can be played anytime

---

## Wild Draw Four

### Effect
- Choose the next color
- Next player draws 4 cards
- Next player loses their turn

### Restriction
- Can only be played if you have NO card matching the current color

---

# Calling UNO

When you have:
- exactly 1 card left

You must say:
- `"UNO"`

---

# UNO Penalty

If another player catches you before the next player starts their turn:
- you draw 2 cards

---

# Winning

The round ends when:
- a player has no cards left

That player wins the round.

---

# Scoring (Optional)

## Number Cards
Worth face value:
- `0–9` points

---

## Action Cards
Worth:
- `20` points each

Includes:
- Skip
- Reverse
- Draw Two

---

## Wild Cards
Worth:
- `50` points each

Includes:
- Wild
- Wild Draw Four

---

# Important Official Rules

## Stacking Draw Cards

Official UNO rules:
- NOT allowed

### Example
- Cannot stack `Draw Two` on another `Draw Two`

---

## Wild Draw Four Challenge

A player may challenge a `Wild Draw Four`.

### If Illegal
- offending player draws 4 cards

### If Legal
- challenger draws 6 cards

---

# Common House Rules (Not Official)

Many people play with unofficial rules such as:
- stacking draw cards
- jump-in
- swapping hands
- continuous stacking
